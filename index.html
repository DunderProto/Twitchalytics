What is Twitchalytics?
Twitch is an online streaming service where people can broadcast themselves playing a game or any other activity, usually with commentary. Over the past few years, Twitch has become the world's leading streaming platform, boasting more than 3 million active monthly creators and over 15 million average daily streamers as of 2020.
Twitchalytics serves as an online application to provide data analysis on viewership, the growth of Twitch, games being played, and languages that streamers are speaking. These data points are represented by various visual graphs.
Average Concurrent Viewers
The average number of concurrent viewers increased by nearly four times the amount from the start of 2016, reaching over 2 million individual viewers. In April 2020, there was a 52% surge in popularity which was likely caused by the global pandemic at the time where people spent more time at home.
